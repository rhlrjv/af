[Breaking News] SoC Lecturers to Wear Darth Vader costume while teaching

"Darth Vader is awesome and so are SoC lecturers. So why shouldn't we double the awesomeness by letting lecturers teach in a Darth Vader costume?"- a student once asked us. This really made us think and after deep consideration, we have decided to enforce a regulation starting from the next academic year requiring professors to wear Darth Vader costumes to lectures. Additionally, lecturers will also be given light sabers to aid their teaching.

The regulation has been introduced with the objective of ensuring that the "cool" factor of our lecturers is never diminished. Whenever students have a doubt like "Is my professor awesome?", they will look at the costume he is wearing and never ever doubt his awesomeness. The lecturers are certainly very excited about their new role and we are very sure that the response from students will be positive as well.

If you have any comments or suggestions about this, please share your thoughts with your professors during your next lecture or post them on your IVLE forum :)

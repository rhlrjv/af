[Breaking News] CORS Bidding System to Change Starting AY 2014-15

For ages, NUS Students have been struggling to bid for their modules. But not anymore! NUS SoC, has finally found a solution to make CORS bidding better. After a lot of research and debate, it was agreed upon that students will now be able to use CANDY CRUSH points to bid for their modules.

Why Candy Crush points?
Our research showed that students love Candy Crush and it is their favorite pass time. So we thought, why not use their hard earned points and put them to good use? Students struggle to save up their CORS points but they would no longer have to worry about it. They just need to be play Candy Crush, collect as many points as they need and bid for the modules they love.

We are very excited to see how this is received among the students. This scheme will be implemented starting AY 2014/2015.

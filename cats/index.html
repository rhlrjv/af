[Breaking News] CORS Bidding System to Change Starting AY 2014-15

Students have increasingly started liking cats and for many of them, they are more than just pets. A lot of students we interviewed enlightened us that they think of cats as their good luck charm. They sneakily bring in pictures of cats to exams just to fare well or draw a cat on their exam sheets for good luck.

We at SoC are always working towards making student life better, and if cats help students perform better, we think they should be allowed in the exam halls. Hence after deep consideration, the management has decided to allow students to bring in their beloved kitties and ace their exams.

We are very excited to see how this is received among the students. This scheme will be implemented starting AY 2014/2015.
